# Lab 1: Set Up the Notebook

**Goal:** Create your first notebook in NotebookLM and upload the governing
reference document for the Unit Rekod Perubatan, Hospital Daerah Seri Aman.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Use **public documents only** for this exercise. Do not
> upload confidential documents or patient data to a personal account.

---

## Steps

1. Go to `https://notebooklm.google.com` and sign in with your Google account.

2. Click **Create** / **New notebook**.

3. Click the **Add sources** button.

   ![Add sources in NotebookLM](img/01-add-sources.png)

4. Download the PRIMARY governing reference for the Hospital Daerah Seri Aman
   scenario:
   - Open [`public-references.md`](../public-references.md) and locate the
     **PRIMARY** document: **"Garis Panduan Pengendalian dan Pengurusan Rekod
     Perubatan Pesakit di Fasiliti KKM"** (MOH Medical Development Division).
   - Download the PDF using the direct link or the stable fallback portal link
     listed there.

   > **Note:** If you are using your own authorized public or de-identified
   > document instead, you may substitute it at this step.

5. Upload those documents to NotebookLM:
   - Choose **Upload** and select the downloaded PDF file, **or**
   - Use the **Website / Link** option and paste the link directly (if
     supported).

6. Wait for NotebookLM to process (index) the documents. You will see a list
   of sources in the left panel.

> **Tip:** Give your notebook a meaningful name, e.g. "Rujukan Rekod Perubatan
> — Hospital Seri Aman".

---

## Expected outcome

A notebook containing the **MOH records management guideline** as its primary
source, processed and listed in the sources panel (left). NotebookLM is ready
to answer questions about records handling at Hospital Daerah Seri Aman.
